Pork Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. The dish consists of mainly of pig's parts such as minced pork meat, ears and face. Chicken liver, onions and chilli peppers are also added.

It has a rich, salty and meaty taste and is delicious. It is the perfect dish to have with drinks for when you gather with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the sisig

  • 1 lb. of pig ears
  • 1 lb. of pig snout
  • 1 lb. of pork belly
  • 1 quart of water
  • 2 diced onions
  • 4 thumbs of ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 3 bay leaves

For the dressing

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of sukang iloko
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of liver spread
  • 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon of Knorr Liquid Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boiling the meat - Put the pig ears, pork belly, bay leaves, 2 teaspoons of salt and 1 quart of water in a cooking pot. Apply some heat and boil all of this for 1 hour. Then take the ingredients out and drain the water.
  2. Grillling the pig parts - Starting heating up the grill. ll. Put the meat you prepared earlier on the grill, and cook them for 5 minutes. Then turn them to the other side, and repeat the previous step. Transfer the cooked pig parts to a separate container, and let it cool down.
  3. Preparing the sisig dressing - Combine all the dressing ingredients together in a bowl. Make sure to mix them thoroughly
  4. Tossing the meat and dressing together - Now chop up the pig parts you grilled earlier. Get them in small pieces, and place these in a large mixing bowl. Pour in your dressing mixture. Then just toss everything together until they are all well-blended.
  5. How to serve - Transfer the pork sisig to a plate and serve with some warm, freshly cooked rice. Enjoy!
