Pork Adobo

Description

Considered by some as the pride of Filipino cuisine, Pork Adobo is definitely a favorite among many around the world. For this, we cook pork slices in a perfect mix of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic to make this savoury and sour.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons of vinegar
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of peppercorns
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinate for at least 1 hour
  2. Heat the pot and put in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic into the pot
  4. Add water, whole pepper corn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
  5. Put in the vinegar and simmer for 12-15 minutes
  6. Add salt (as much as you need)
  7. Serve while hot, enjoy!
